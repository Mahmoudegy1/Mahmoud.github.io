Identity

Egypt's National Symbols & Famous People

Explore the rich cultural identity and notable figures of Egypt.

National Symbols

Flag

The flag of Egypt consists of three horizontal stripes of red, white, and black, with the national emblem, the Eagle of Saladin, centered in the white stripe.

Animal

The national animal of Egypt is the Steppe Eagle, often associated with the country's cultural significance.

Flower

The Lotus Flower, with its cultural and symbolic significance, is considered the national flower of Egypt.

Famous People

Politician

Anwar Sadat, the third President of Egypt, played a key role in the Arab-Israeli conflict and won the Nobel Peace Prize for his efforts.

Entertainer

Umm Kulthum, known as the "Star of the East," was a legendary Egyptian singer, songwriter, and actress, renowned for her contributions to Arabic music.

Writer

Naguib Mahfouz, an Egyptian writer and Nobel laureate, is best known for his novels exploring themes of existentialism, politics, and society.
