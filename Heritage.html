Culture and Economy Overview

Get to know Egypt's main cultural and economic aspects and impacts

Culture

Music

1-Rich musical heritage with classical Arabic, folk, and contemporary genres.
2-Instruments like oud and qanun are prevalent

Art

1-Ancient Egyptian art influences modern creativity.
2-Islamic art and contemporary expressions contribute to a vibrant scene.

Dance

1-Iconic belly dance ("Raqs Sharqi") and traditional folk dances.
2-Cultural diversity reflected in dance forms.

Cuisine

1-Diverse culinary traditions with dishes like koshari and falafel.
2-Middle Eastern and Mediterranean influences.

Crafts

1-Skilled artisans in pottery, weaving, and glassblowing.
2-Revival of ancient techniques, like papyrus art.

Traditional Vocations

1-Agriculture, fishing, and traditional crafts.
2-Growing significance of tourism-related occupations.

Economy

Main Industries

1-Agriculture, manufacturing, and tourism play pivotal roles.
2-Energy sector, including the Suez Canal, contributes substantially.

Trading Partners

1-Engages with the EU, the U.S., China, and Arab nations.
2-Strategic location of the Suez Canal enhances global trade ties.

Currency

1-Official currency is the Egyptian Pound (EGP).

Economic Challenges

1-Faces challenges like high population growth and youth unemployment.
2-Prioritizing economic diversification and infrastructure development.
