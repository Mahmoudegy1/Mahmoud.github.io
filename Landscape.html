Climate

Egypt has a desert climate, characterized by hot summers and mild winters. The country experiences very little rainfall, and temperatures can be extreme, especially in the inland areas.

Famous Landmarks and Sites

Explore iconic landmarks such as the Pyramids of Giza, the Sphinx, Luxor Temple, and the Valley of the Kings. Egypt is home to a rich history and ancient wonders.

Important Holidays

Celebrate key holidays in Egypt, including Eid al-Fitr, Ramadan, and National Day. These festivities showcase the cultural diversity and traditions of the country.

Time Zone

Egypt operates on Eastern European Time (EET), which is UTC+2. The country does not observe Daylight Saving Time.
